We are using anatomical features of annual rings and vessels in oak trees to reconstruct flood events in eastern Iowa. Funded by Office of Undergraduate Research, University of Iowa, 2025-2026. This research has been identified as an outstanding project to be featured in [photography](https://x.com/uiowa/status/1999252925940433160). Personnel: Feng Wang (PI), Paige Liebrecht, Matt Dannenberg
